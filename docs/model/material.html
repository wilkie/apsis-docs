Apsis::Model:: Class Material

Source

model/material.h

Methods

    shininess - Retrieve the shininess of the material. This affects how specular light will reflect.
    emission - Retrieve the type of glow the material has. The glow is the color of light that emits from the material.
    diffuseIntensity - Retrieve the diffuse intensity of the material. This affects how light will shade the object.
    specularIntensity - Retrieve the specular intensity of the material. This affects how light will reflect off of the object.
    ambientIntensity - Retrieve the ambient intensity of the material. This affects how ambient light (light that hits from all directions) will shade the object. Ambient light is an unnatural light.
